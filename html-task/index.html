What is Artificial Intelligence ?

Artificial Intelligence refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, reason, learn, and solve problems. It includes a variety of subfields such as:

  • Machine Learning (ML): Teaching machines to learn from data.
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP): Enabling machines to understand human language.
  • Computer Vision: Helping machines see and understand the world around them.
  • Robotics: Making machines capable of performing complex tasks .

Applications of AI:

AI is already being used in a wide range of applications, including:

  • Healthcare: Diagnosing diseases, providing personalized treatment plans.
  • Finance: Fraud detection, personalized investment portfolios .

Challenges and Ethical Considerations:

While AI holds immense potential, it also presents several challenges and ethical considerations, including:

  • Bias: AI systems can inadvertently learn and reflect biases present in the training data.
